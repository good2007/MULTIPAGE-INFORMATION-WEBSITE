About BROOKLYN ROYAL SCHOOL

History and Heritage

Founded in Nairobi in 1973, BROOKLYN ROYAL SCHOOL was created to serve talented boys who could not otherwise afford a strong education. The school combines academic achievement with a caring home environment to build lives of purpose.

Academic Excellence

Students follow a broad curriculum with strong emphasis on mathematics, science, languages, and information technology. Regular revision, mentorship, and individual support help every student achieve excellent national exam results.

Life at the Centre

Boarding life includes daily study sessions, sports, cultural clubs, and community service. Students learn teamwork, resilience, and leadership while living in a supportive family atmosphere.

What Makes Us Special

  • Free education and boarding for deserving students.
  • Strong mentorship from experienced teachers and alumni.
  • Deep commitment to integrity, discipline, and service.
  • Graduates who become leaders in business, public service, and technology.

Our Journey

1973 - Foundation

Established to provide quality education for gifted boys from disadvantaged backgrounds in Kenya.

1980s - Growth

Expanded classrooms, dormitories and support programs while strengthening academic and extracurricular offerings.

1990s - Innovation

Introduced technology, science labs, and modern teaching methods that prepared students for a changing world.

2000s - Recognition

Earned recognition as a leading institution in character formation and academic performance nationally and internationally.

Today

Continuing the legacy of excellence by nurturing future leaders in education, public service, and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

🎓 Educational Excellence

🌟 Character Development

Fostering responsible citizens through values of respect, courage, compassion, and accountability.

🤝 Equal Opportunity

Creating access to education for talented boys regardless of financial means, with full boarding and supportive care.
